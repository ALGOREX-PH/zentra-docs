**Zentra** lets developers build AI agents that can trigger Stellar payments only
after proving, in zero knowledge, that they followed private, user-defined
policies. A Soroban contract verifies the proof, checks it against the agent's
authoritative on-chain state, settles the payment, and emits a verifiable receipt.

> Let agents act. Make them prove it. — **No proof, no payment.**

## The one question

Agentic finance already has answers for *who is this agent?* (identity) and
*what is it allowed to do?* (permissions). Zentra answers the third:

> Did this specific action, right now, actually obey the rules — without exposing
> the private details?

Proof replaces surveillance. Every action becomes a proof, every proof a receipt,
every receipt reputation.
